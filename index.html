Character Class Selection // Terra A.D.

You survived the Rite of Passage. Now choose who you will become in the wasteland.

Classes Pick Your Class
⚔️ Sentinel
Combat

Sentinel

The Warrior of the Wastes

Sentinels are the toughest and hardest-hitting characters in the group. They excel at combat and have a natural talent for identifying and wielding ancient technology — laser pistols, grenades, power armor. Reliable at low levels, devastating once armed with artifacts.

Best ForPlayers who want to be the group's muscle and tech weapon specialist.
🗡️ Rover
Stealth / Utility

Rover

The Scout and Scavenger

Quick, sneaky, and street-smart. Rovers backstab enemies, move silently, find traps before they kill everyone, and scavenge better than anyone else. They reward clever, opportunistic play over brute force.

Best ForPlayers who like playing smart and picking their moments rather than charging in headfirst.
🔮 Shaman
Tech Magic / Utility

Shaman

Keeper of Ancient Knowledge

Shamans treat technology as sacred relics and ancient AIs as gods. They commune with artificial intelligences and ancient systems in ways no other class can. They fill the role of magic user in MCC — mysterious, powerful, and unpredictable.

Best ForPlayers who want a spiritual, mysterious character with unique utility and power.
💉 Healer
Support

Healer

The Medic

The Healer keeps the group alive — and in a game as lethal as MCC, that matters enormously. Straightforward to play, though healing uses are limited per day so resource management is key. Not flashy, but genuinely vital.

Best ForPlayers who don't mind a support role and want to be the reason everyone else survives.
☢️ Mutant
Mutations / Wild Card

Mutant

Unpredictable. Dangerous. Unique.

Pure humans touched by radiation and quantum fields. Mutants have randomly rolled mutations — some powerful, some strange, some outright dangerous. No two Mutants are alike. Your mutations define your character in ways nothing else can replicate.

Best ForPlayers who love randomness, surprises, and a truly one-of-a-kind character.
🐾 Manimal
Mutations / Identity

Manimal

Human-Animal Hybrid

Part human, part animal — you choose your animal type and gain mutations and traits tied to that creature. More focused than a straight Mutant. If you're a bear-man, you feel like a bear-man. Flavorful, physical, and fun to roleplay.

Best ForPlayers who want a strong, distinctive identity baked into their character from the start.
🌿 Plantient
Mutations / Exotic

Plantient

Sentient Plant Creature

Strange, resilient, and unlike anything else at the table. Plantients have a unique mutation set rooted in plant biology — vines, spores, photosynthesis and more. Genuinely weird in the best way, with a playstyle no other class can match.

Best ForPlayers who want to play something truly alien and different.

⚠️ A Note on Tone

Mutant Crawl Classics is gonzo and lethal. Death is real, danger is everywhere, and that's part of the fun. The mutation-based classes (Mutant, Manimal, Plantient) are wild and unpredictable — exciting but sometimes chaotic. The conventional classes (Sentinel, Rover) tend to be more reliable survivors early on. There's no wrong choice — just pick what sounds fun to play. Terra A.D. doesn't care if you're ready.
